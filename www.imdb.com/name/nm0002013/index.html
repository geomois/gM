Charles Coburn (I) (1877–1961)

Actor | Soundtrack
A cigar-smoking, monocled, swag-bellied character actor known for his Old South manners and charm. In 1918 he and his first wife formed the Coburn Players and appeared on Broadway in many plays. With her death in 1937, he accepted a Hollywood contract and began making films at the age of sixty. See full bio »

Born:

June 19, 1877 in Savannah, Georgia, USA

Died:

August 30, 1961 (age 84) in New York City, New York, USA
